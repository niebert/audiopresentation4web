- Tutorial

AudioSlides4Web was created as a online alternative for PanDocElectron. See AudioSlides4Web in Wikiversity for further details or the AudioSlides4Web-Tutorial. This tool will copy you audio files in the subfolder /audio/ and the slides e.g. as PNG-images or GIF-animations into the folder /images/. You can also load a PDF-File as source for the slides with "Add Slides"-Button. Adapt the basename of your audio files and images to the base name in the interface below.
